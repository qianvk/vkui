# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the vkui community a respectful, harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be welcoming, patient, and constructive.
- Discuss technical decisions and observable behavior, not personal attributes.
- Respect differing experience levels and platform constraints.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Keep private information private.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, and
publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communication that violate this
code. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban. Report conduct
issues privately using the contact in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); reports will be reviewed promptly
and handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
